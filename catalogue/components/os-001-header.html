Header os-001

Also known as: Navigation bar, Nav, Top bar

Fixed site navigation with brand, links, CTA, and mobile drawer.

Variants
default

Static height. No scroll reaction. Simple border bottom.

shrink

Reduces height on scroll. Brand scales down. Background blurs to frosted glass. Subtle shadow appears.

How it works
Sticky. Opens at full height showing the brand and navigation prominently. On scroll, the header shrinks — height reduces, brand scales down, and the background gains a frosted-glass blur. Remains visible throughout the page. On mobile, navigation collapses into a hamburger that opens a full-width drawer.
