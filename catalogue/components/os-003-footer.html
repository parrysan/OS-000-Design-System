Multi-column link grid with section headers and legal bar below. See main example above.

How it works
Sits at the bottom of every page. The simple variant is a single copyright line on a dark background. The columns variant adds a grid of categorised links above the copyright — typically navigation, legal, and resource links. Column count adapts from 2 on mobile to 4 on desktop.
